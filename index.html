Homemade Soft Pretzels

Ingredients:

  • 4 tsp dry active yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup warm water
  • 5 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbs vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup baking soda
  • 4 cup hot water
  • 1/4 cup pretzel salt

Procedure:

  1. Dissolve yeast, 1 tsp sugar, and 1 1/4 cup water. Let stand until creamy. (10 min.)
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add oil and yeast mix. Knead until smooth. (7-8 min.) Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover and let rise until double in size. (1 hr.)
  3. Preheat oven to 450 F, Grease 2 large baking sheets.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Turn dough onto a floured surface and divide into 12 equal pieces. Roll each strip and shape into a pretzel. Dip each pretzel in the baking soda water. Salt if desired.
  5. Bake until browned. (8 min)
